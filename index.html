Who We Are – Crafting Quality, Sustaining Communities

Mossphilo International Private Limited is a pioneering food processing company headquartered in Nashik, Maharashtra. Established to meet India’s growing demand for processed foods, we specialize in producing high-quality, hygienic, and sustainable products, including packaged snacks, RTE meals, and value-added agricultural goods. Our vision is to become a global leader in food processing while supporting Indian farmers and promoting sustainable practices.

Our Values:

  • Quality: Adhering to stringent FSSAI and international standards.
  • Sustainability: Reducing environmental impact through green packaging and energy-efficient processes.
  • Community: Empowering local farmers and creating jobs in rural India.

Our Journey:

  • Founded in 2025, Mossphilo is backed by a team of food technologists, industry experts, and passionate entrepreneurs.
  • Leveraging India’s rich agricultural heritage and government initiatives like Make in India and Atmanirbhar Bharat.

Taking Indian Flavors to the World

Mossphilo International is committed to showcasing India’s culinary heritage on the global stage. With a robust export strategy, we aim to deliver high-quality processed foods to international markets, leveraging India’s position as a leading exporter of agricultural products (valued at USD 43.2 billion in 2022-23, per APEDA).

Key Export Markets:

  • Middle East: High demand for spices, RTE meals, and frozen foods in UAE, Saudi Arabia, and Qatar.
  • North America and Europe: Targeting Indian diaspora and health-conscious consumers with organic and clean-label products.

Export Strategy:

  • Certifications: Compliance with HACCP, ISO 22000, and FDA standards for seamless exports.
  • Partnerships: Collaborating with APEDA and international distributors for market access.
  • Logistics: Leveraging India’s cold chain infrastructure and ports (e.g., JNPT, Mumbai) for efficient shipping.
  • Customized Offerings: Tailoring products (e.g., spice blends, halal-certified RTE meals) to meet regional preferences.

Why Choose Mossphilo for Exports?

  • High-quality, authentic Indian products with consistent supply.
  • Competitive pricing and scalable production capacity.
  • Strong focus on sustainability and ethical sourcing.
